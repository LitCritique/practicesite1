Welcome to my portfolio

I just completed my 16th year in education. I've taught in both public schools and charter schools, and I am currently teaching English 10 and AP Seminar English 10 at Colgan High School .

I started teaching middle school English to grades 6-8 Gifted in Winter Haven, Florida. After 8 years, I moved to a charter school in Lakeland and taught as the only 7th grade English teacher. Three years later, I moved to 9th grade English and 9th grade Adv. After Covid, my husband and I decided to pack up and move to Virginia. Now, I teach at a high school with a focus on fine arts and I couldn't be happier.

I earned my BA in English and Secondary Education in 2007, my Master's in Educational Leadership in 2016, and I am currently working on a Master's in English Literature.
